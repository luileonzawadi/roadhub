FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Roadhub learning system.

How is Roadhub different from university education?

Roadhub bridges the gap between theory and real engineering practice. While universities focus on academic foundations, Roadhub provides hands-on experience with real-world projects, industry tools, and professional mentorship from practicing engineers.

Who are the instructors and mentors?

Our instructors and mentors are professional engineers with years of hands-on experience in their respective fields. They bring real-world insights, industry best practices, and practical knowledge from global projects.

What disciplines and topics are covered?

We cover 13 engineering disciplines including Mechanical (HVAC, Plumbing, Fire Fighting), Electrical (Power, Light Current, BMS), Architecture (Design, Landscape, Interior), and Civil (Structure, Infrastructure). Each discipline includes specialized learning paths.

How does the practical task system work?

Our 6-step practical task system guides you through real engineering scenarios: Purpose, Foundation, The Task, Task Input, Task Steps, and Practice. Each step builds on the previous one to give you complete project experience.

Is the platform suitable for beginners?

Yes. Roadhub is designed for students, fresh graduates, and practicing engineers at all levels. Our structured learning paths adapt to your experience level, whether you are building foundations or expanding expertise.

Are You Ready for the Real Engineering Job Market?

With Roadhub, you become truly ready for the real engineering job market by gaining hands-on experience through structured tasks, working on real projects from global markets, and learning within an interactive system that seamlessly integrates theory with practical application.
